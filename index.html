Muhammad Ali Jinnah

1876 - 1948

The founder of Pakistan

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah was the visionary leader who founded Pakistan and gave direction to the destiny of millions. He devoted his life to the struggle for the political rights and freedom of Muslims in the subcontinent. His leadership was rooted in justice, equality, and the supremacy of law. Through patience, wisdom, and unwavering determination, he turned a challenging dream into reality. He firmly believed that unity, faith, and discipline were essential for building a strong nation. Quaid-e-Azam led with honesty, moral courage, and a deep sense of responsibility. Even in the most difficult times, he never compromised on principles.
